Project Background

Today, delivery agents include shipping employees, couriers, and freelancers on platforms like Uber Eats. E-commerce growth, driven by companies like Amazon and Walmart, has heightened demand for delivery services. In Bhutan, technology adoption and government support for entrepreneurship are fueling demand for efficient delivery solutions in the growing e-commerce sector.

Problem Statement

People who stay far away from each other face the problem like they are not able to send or get their important things within a short period of time. Delivering items via post or any other way takes too much time and wastes the person's time.

The Goal

To build an application that will enable the citizens of Bhutan to deliver or receive parcels/things within a short period of time.

Batanical Bliss

Project Background

Botanical Bliss is a flower-selling initiative focused on providing fresh, beautiful floral arrangements and individual flowers to customers. The project is inspired by the growing demand for high-quality flowers for various occasions, including weddings, birthdays, corporate events, and personal gifts. The floral industry continues to thrive as consumers seek out unique and meaningful ways to express emotions and celebrate milestones. Botanical Bliss aims to capture this market by offering a range of flowers with an emphasis on quality, freshness, and customer satisfaction.

Problem Statement

The flower industry faces challenges in maintaining the freshness and quality of flowers throughout the supply chain, from sourcing to final sale. Customers frequently encounter issues like limited variety, short flower shelf-life, and inconsistency in quality. Additionally, there is a need for convenient and reliable access to flowers, as consumers are increasingly looking for flexible purchasing options. Botanical Bliss seeks to address these issues by establishing a system that ensures freshness, consistency, and availability, thereby enhancing the customer experience and reducing waste.

The Goal

The goal of Botanical Bliss is to provide high-quality, fresh flowers with convenient access and exceptional customer service, meeting diverse customer needs while promoting sustainable practices.
